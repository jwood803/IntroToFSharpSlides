My first look at F#


let rec cast<'a> (myList: obj list) =
	match myList with
	| head::tail ->
		match head with
		| :? 'a as a -> a::(cast tail)
		| _ -> cast tail
	| [] -> []

Why F#?

  • Rapid prototyping with the F# Interactive
  • Fewer bugs

    • Units of Measure
  • Interoperability
    • Other languages than just .NET languages
